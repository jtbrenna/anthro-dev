The Anthropology Club

With the help of a faculty advisor, the Anthropology Club is a student-run organization providing a diversity of anthropological activities such as:

  • Field trips to Montreal and Boston to visit the newest museum exhibits.
  • Guest speakers.
  • Film showings.
  • Competition in the Annual Northeast Open Atlatl Championship
  • Presenting papers and posters at and attending conferences such as the Northeastern Anthropological Association and American Anthropological Association meetings.
  • Serving local organizations such as the Vermont Film Festival, ECHO, and the Fleming Museum.

Join us! Visit us at ~anthclub or e-mail anthclub@uvm.edu.
