Work Study

The Department of Anthropology at the University of Vermont offers positions to many students who are eligible for the Federal Work Study Program

Examples of past positions include:

  • Research Assistant
  • Teaching Assistant
  • Program Assistant
  • Webmaster
  • Archaeological Illustrator

FWS is a wonderful opportunity to get paid while working in a field directly relevant to your future career. You can find open positions on-campus and at off-campus service sites through the Student Employment Office website, but you should also feel free to contact any of our professors to see if a position can be created to serve your mutual interests.
