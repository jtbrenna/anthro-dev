Study Abroad Funding

Finances preventing your from studying abroad? Consider these options:

    The Kleinknecht Fund: A department alum has made money available for occasional use. Students can apply for small amounts to support research, conference travel, etc. For more information, email Prof. Gordon or consult the Department Chair.

    The Jim Petersen Fund: Named in honor of department chair Jim Petersen who died in 2005, funds are available to support attendance at a UVM archaeological field school.
