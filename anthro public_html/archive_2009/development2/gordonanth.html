Robert Gordon, rgordon@zoo.uvm.edu Professor, came to UVM in 1979. He received his B.A. and M.A. from the University of Stellenbosch and his Ph.D. from the University of Illinois. His field work has been primarily in Southern Africa and New Guinea. In Namibia he studied mineworkers and Bushman genocide. His theoretical interests include politics and law, visual and urban anthropology, development in third world and culture change.
