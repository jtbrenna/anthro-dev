Jeanne Shea, jlshea@zoo.uvm.edu Assistant Professor, came to UVM in 1998. She received her B.A. in Asian Studies from Dartmouth College and her M.A. and Ph.D. in Social Anthropology from Harvard University. Her research interests focus on China, the U.S.A., health, gender, ethnicity, class, the lifecourse, generations, economic development and social change. To learn more about Professor Shea and the courses she teaches, visit her web site at http://www.uvm.edu/~jlshea/.
