A gentle remembrance in pictures and words...

On August 13th, 2005, Jim Petersen was shot and killed while conducting fieldwork in Brazil, an area he had worked in for many years. We will miss him. His kindness, humor, gentleness and hard-working spirit provide inspiration for many. As a mentor and teacher, he touched countless numbers of students and colleagues all over the world.

This is a remembrance in words and pictures.

UVM Anthropology, 2005
